I have been producing, editing, and presenting podcasts for over 18 years. My first podcast, [**The Bugcast**](https://thebugcast.org/), which won the [European Podcast Award for UK Personality in 2012](https://thebugcast.org/2013/03/25/european-podcast-award-2012-uk-personality/), is still going strong after over 900 episodes.

Most of the podcasts that I am involved with are a part of the [**Other Side Podcast Network**](https://otherside.network/), of which I am a proud founder member.

## Podcasts ...

Here are the shows that have made up my podcasting career since 2008.  Although the active count is currently six, it's probably more accurate to say that I'm actively involved in four or five at this time.
